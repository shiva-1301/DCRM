DCRM Diagnostics

1

Data Preparation

Export your DCRM measurement data as a standard CSV. Ensure common channels like Coil Current (C1) and Contact Travel (T1) are properly indexed.

2

Model Execution

Upload your file to the 'New Analysis' engine. Our AI will process the multi-vector input to identify fault patterns in milliseconds.

Validation Loop: For every prediction, provide feedback by confirming or correcting the result. This directly improves the system's accuracy over time.

SOS Protocols

When encountering hardware failures or ambiguous results that require immediate senior intervention:

1

Escalation

Navigate to SOS Request, select the relevant fault category, and provide a high-fidelity description of the field observations.

2

Tracking

Monitor the status (Pending/Resolved) directly from your dashboard. Admins will update the ticket once a resolution is identified.
